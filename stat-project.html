Project Details

statistical data analysis

In this project, I was asked to check the probability of a baby weighing under 2.5 kg with the given data. Also was asked include top 1% and bottom 1% result as well. First checked if the weights can be assumed to follow a normal distrubition by plotting data in a histogram, then used Emperical Rule to confirm normal distribution. Then, calculated the probability of a baby weighing 2.5 kg or less. Finally estimated the values for top 1% and 99% cumulative probabilites and counted the number of births over and under the thresholds
